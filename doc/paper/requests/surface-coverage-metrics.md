request_id: surface-coverage-metrics
source: gatekeeper-review

## Paper Claim
Vertex-only coverage does not establish continuous mesh-surface coverage. The paper needs a surface-sampled coverage audit for generated FR3 outputs before making conservative collision statements.

## Task Type
experiment

- Optional diagnostic figures for the worst uncovered link and mode if generated by the script.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Use deterministic mesh-surface sampling with a recorded seed.
- Use at least 10,000 surface samples per evaluated link when the mesh has enough area to support that count.
- Evaluate convex, sphere single/default, and capsule single/default/high_detail from the mode-comparison artifact.
- Report unsupported or missing modes explicitly rather than silently skipping them.
- RESULT.md states whether surface sampling confirms, weakens, or contradicts vertex-only coverage conclusions.
